Banana Berry Smoothie

Description

Enjoy a refreshing berry smoothie to enjoy on a hot summer day, which also can act as a healthy treat! These ingredients are easy to come across, making this smoothie easy and convenient. You can choose to include all berries you typically find in a berry mix or select your own. This recipe assumes that a mix will include blackberries, blueberries, and raspberries. You will need a blender of some sort in order to make this smoothie!

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 1/2 cup frozen berry mix
  • 1 cup apple juice
  • 1/4 cup ice

Steps

  1. Add ice cream, bananas, berries, apple juice, and ice to blender.
  2. Blend until no individual ingredients are visibly whole. Serve and enjoy!
